Got Questions?

Your Burning Inquiries Answered

We've got the lowdown on how we operate. Ask away!

How does this work?

Restaurants list extra food, volunteers pick it up for shelters.

Is the food safe?

We prioritize food safety with strict guidelines and tracking.

Who are the volunteers?

Dedicated individuals passionate about reducing waste and feeding others.

What kind of food?

Any edible surplus from restaurants, from pastries to full meals.

How is impact measured?

Our dashboard shows meals donated and waste diverted.

Can I donate food?

Restaurants can list surplus; individuals can volunteer for pickups.

What about AI?

AI optimizes pickup routes and predicts surplus for better planning.

Is it free?

The service is free for shelters and NGOs receiving food.
